Theme of ICAPIE 2021

Design, Materials and Manufacturing for Sustainable Environment



The paper may be focused on the following areas, but not limited to these areas.


  1. Ceramics, Composites, Polymers and Biomaterials

  2. Heat Treatment, Microstructure and Materials Properties

  3. 3D, Magnetic and Functional Graded Materials, Nanomaterials, Multifunctional and Smart Materials and Emerging Trends

  4. Automation in Manufacturing, Rapid Manufacturing Technologies, Prototyping, Remanufacturing, Additive Manufacturing, Advanced Manufacturing Processes and Nanomanufacturing

  5. Wear, Tribology, and Surface Engineering, Crack propagation and fracture mechanics

  6. Non – Conventional, Metal Forming, Metal Joining and Micro Machining

  7. Green fuel production, Green and Sustainable Manufacturing, Green Tribology and Green Mobility, Green supply chain management

  8. E-commerce, Production Economics, ICT entrepreneurship, Internet of Things, Data Analytics and Data Science

  9. Advanced Management Techniques
    1. Supply Chain Management, Industrial Engineering Managements, Quality Management Systems
    2. Materials, Project and sustainable Production Management
    3. Sustainable Development

  10. Product and Graphics Design, Modelling and Simulations

  11. Production & Industrial Engineering, Operations Research
